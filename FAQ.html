Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is 7earnings ?

7earnings is an online investment platform designed to give Nigerians an extra source of income.


2. How can i register to start earning ?

To register you need to pay N1400 via with your atm card or purchase an e-pin worth N1,400 which will be inserted on the registration page, without it your registration won't be processed by our servers.


3. I tried registering but i'm getting error messages, what can i do ?

Your registration will not be processed if you have incorrect details which could be : incorrect pin, your username and email are not the same, one of the fields is empty etc. So please endeavor all your details are correct and your email and username are in lowercase.


4. How do i earn ?

After successful registration and login, you need to start clicking the earn buttons on your profile which will give you N10 per click and for each person you refer you get N500.


5. I referred someone, how do i get rewarded ?

Once someone registers with your username as referrer on the registration page, he/she is linked to you. But to earn your reward, the person has to login and activate you by clicking activate referral on his dashboard after which he fills in your details as referrer and his username. After that, you are to go to www.7earnings.com.ng/referral/his_username.html (eg. www.7earnings.com.ng/referral/doris@gmail.com.html) and fill the form to collect the reward. Once you fill the form, you will be redirected back to your dashboard where you notice that the earnings has been added.


6. How do i withdraw ?

To withdraw after you have gotten a minimum of N3,000 for clicking or you have a minimum refferal earning of N500. The withdrawal button will be activated. You then need to fill the withdrawal page with your details and submit. After that await payment on saturday of the week.


7. When can i place withdrawal ?

You can practically do so after you have a minimum of N3,000 but to be sure you receive your alert on the saturday after withdrawal is placed, you need to place the request from Wednesday 12:00am - Friday 11:59pm.


8. After withdrawing for the first time , can i do so again ?

To do so you need to get at least 2 refferals and continue clicking to get a minimum of N3,000 then place withdrawal.


9. How do I watch a youtube video?

Once you click watch video button on your dashboard, you will be given youtube videos to watch which you must completely watch. After successfully watching it, you will be rewarded.


10. Are there other ways i can earn ?

Yes, every week the person with the highest number or refferals is rewarded with an extra cash of N2,000.


11. When do i click the finish button ?

You should only click the finish button after you have withdrawn and received payment and want to start another session.


12. How is the income being paid to me weekly generated ?

Generally,we make our money from traffic and ads , from such revenue money is paid to our users.


13. Which users are likely to be paid first ?

We pay our users with referrals first starting from the ones with higher referrals to the ones without referrals. So if you placed a withdrawal and you are not paid that week,kindly await the next week and place a withdrawal again as it means revenue was exhausted.


14. How can i start a new session ?

Whether you were paid or not after you placed withdrawal, you will be required to start a new session before you can click more. If you don't your click buttons won't work. So if you place withdrawal and you want to start a new session kindly click the button on your profile. Alternatively you can decide not to click it if you think your click earnings is big enough for next withdrawal. Because once you click start new session your refferal earnings must be wiped off.


15. I started another session after i received my first payment but even after i reached N3,000 from my clicking i cannot withdraw ?

Once you have withdrawn and want to withdraw again, you need to click the finish button on your profile and get a minimum of 2 refferals before your withdrawal button is activated again.

7earnings utilizes your phone storage to store some information and in the case of wiping your phone memory, such data will be lost. In case this happens kindly 7earnings can not do anything and you will be required to start over
